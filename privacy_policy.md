# SundayCrier Privacy Policy

SundayCrier is a personal notification tool used to send calendar updates to a small group of known recipients.

## Information Collected
The system uses:
- Phone numbers (for SMS delivery)
- Email addresses (for email delivery)
- Calendar event data from Google Calendar

## How Information Is Used
Information is used solely to:
- Send scheduled notifications about calendar events
- Deliver messages to intended recipients

No data is sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes.

## Data Sharing
No personal data is shared with third parties except as required to deliver messages (e.g., Twilio for SMS, Google for calendar access).

## Consent
Recipients are manually added and have provided direct consent to receive messages.

## Opt-Out
Recipients can opt out at any time by requesting removal, and their information will be deleted from the system.

## Contact
For any questions or removal requests, contact the system owner directly.
