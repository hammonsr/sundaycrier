# SundayCrier Terms and Conditions

## Program Description
SundayCrier is a personal notification service that sends weekly calendar summaries and updates to a small group of known recipients.

## Message Frequency
Message frequency varies but is typically limited to weekly summaries and occasional updates.

## Message and Data Rates
Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.
